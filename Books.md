# Books

## Technology & Engineering

- **[Designing Data-Intensive Applications](https://dataintensive.net/)** - Martin Kleppmann. The definitive guide to building reliable, scalable data systems. Essential for any engineer working with data.
- **[Accelerate](https://itrevolution.com/product/accelerate/)** - Nicole Forsgren, Jez Humble, Gene Kim. Research-backed practices that predict high software delivery performance.
- **[Team Topologies](https://teamtopologies.com/book)** - Matthew Skelton, Manuel Pais. Modern organizational design patterns for fast flow and reduced cognitive load.
- **[The Phoenix Project](https://itrevolution.com/product/the-phoenix-project/)** - Gene Kim, Kevin Behr, George Spafford. DevOps principles told through an engaging novel. Great entry point.
- **[The DevOps Handbook](https://itrevolution.com/product/the-devops-handbook-second-edition/)** - Gene Kim et al. Practical companion to Accelerate with implementation details.

## Product & Strategy

- **[INSPIRED](https://www.svpg.com/books/inspired-how-to-create-tech-products-customers-love-2nd-edition/)** - Marty Cagan. The product management bible. How the best tech companies build products customers love.
- **[The Lean Startup](https://theleanstartup.com/book)** - Eric Ries. Foundational methodology for building products through validated learning.
- **[Thinking in Systems](https://www.chelseagreen.com/product/thinking-in-systems/)** - Donella Meadows. Essential mental model for understanding complex problems and unintended consequences.

## Management & Leadership

- **[High Output Management](https://www.amazon.com/High-Output-Management-Andrew-Grove/dp/0679762884)** - Andy Grove. Timeless management principles from Intel's legendary CEO. The original.
- **[An Elegant Puzzle](https://press.stripe.com/an-elegant-puzzle)** - Will Larson. Modern engineering management for today's tech orgs. Practical and nuanced.
- **[No Rules Rules](https://www.norulesrules.com/)** - Reed Hastings, Erin Meyer. Netflix's radical culture of freedom and responsibility.
- **[Radical Candor](https://www.radicalcandor.com/the-book)** - Kim Scott. Framework for giving feedback that's both caring and direct.
- **[Measure What Matters](https://www.whatmatters.com/the-book)** - John Doerr. OKRs explained by the person who brought them to Google.

## Personal Development

- **[Deep Work](https://calnewport.com/deep-work-rules-for-focused-success-in-a-distracted-world/)** - Cal Newport. Rules for focused success in a world full of distractions.
- **[Thinking Fast and Slow](https://www.amazon.com/Thinking-Fast-Slow-Daniel-Kahneman/dp/0374533555)** - Daniel Kahneman. How our two thinking systems shape decisions and judgments.
- **[Antifragile](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/176227/antifragile-by-nassim-nicholas-taleb/)** - Nassim Taleb. Things that gain from disorder. Changed how I think about risk and resilience.
- **[The PARA Method](https://www.buildingasecondbrain.com/para)** - Tiago Forte. Simple system for organizing your digital life. I use this daily.
- **[Show Your Work](https://austinkleon.com/show-your-work/)** - Austin Kleon. 10 ways to share your creativity and get discovered. Short and actionable.
- **[Mindset](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/44330/mindset-by-carol-s-dweck-phd/)** - Carol Dweck. The research behind growth vs fixed mindset.

## Health & Longevity

- **[Outlive](https://peterattiamd.com/outlive/)** - Peter Attia. The science of living longer and better. Comprehensive and evidence-based.
